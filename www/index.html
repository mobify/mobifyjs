Remix Your Site for Mobile Devices

Adapting websites for different screens and devices is challenging and time consuming. Use Mobify.js to quickly adapt any website to support any device. Keep the same URL across desktop, mobile and iPad alike.

Mobify.js gives you complete control over how your HTML is interpreted after it has been delivered. Client Side Adaptation allows you to remix content, on the device, on the fly — avoiding the performance challenges of lookahead parsing.

Mobify.js is used by:

Starbucks, Wired, Threadless, Siemens, GQ, Bosch
